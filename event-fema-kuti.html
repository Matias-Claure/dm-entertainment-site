Fema Kuti

Live at the Fonda Theatre

Event Details

Date: Friday, August 8, 2025

Time: 8:00 PM - 10:00 PM

Price Range: $40 - $60

Location: Fonda Theatre, 6126 Hollywood Blvd, Los Angeles, CA

Join Afrobeat legend Fema Kuti and the Positive Force for an electrifying evening at the historic Fonda Theatre. Known for his socially conscious lyrics and unstoppable energy, Fema Kuti carries on the legacy of his father Fela with a modern twist that will have you dancing all night long.

The Fonda Theatre's intimate setting makes this show a must-see for fans of world music. Arrive early to explore Hollywood Boulevard and soak up the vibrant atmosphere before the performance.
