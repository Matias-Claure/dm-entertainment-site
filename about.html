About Us

Learn more about D&M Entertainment and our commitment to unforgettable experiences.

Our Mission

At D&M Entertainment, our mission is to connect people through the power of live events. We curate concerts, festivals and sporting events that celebrate the rich cultural tapestry of Los Angeles. By collaborating with world-class venues and artists, we strive to create experiences that inspire, delight and unite our community.

Our Values

  • Passion: We love what we do and believe in the transformative energy of entertainment.
  • Inclusivity: Our events welcome people from all walks of life and celebrate diverse voices.
  • Excellence: We partner with the best venues and talent to ensure premium experiences.
  • Community: We aim to strengthen the cultural fabric of Los Angeles by bringing people together.

Our Story

Founded in 2018 by lifelong music lovers and sports enthusiasts, D&M Entertainment started as a passion project and quickly grew into a trusted platform for discovering the best events in Los Angeles. Over the years, we've partnered with major venues like the Hollywood Bowl, SoFi Stadium and the Rose Bowl to bring our community closer to the artists and teams they love. We continue to innovate, offering curated recommendations and seamless ticketing experiences so you can focus on making memories.
