Ben Harper & The Innocent Criminals

Live at the Orpheum Theatre

Event Details

Date: Sunday, August 24, 2025

Time: 8:00 PM - 10:00 PM

Price Range: $25 - $150

Location: Orpheum Theatre, 842 S Broadway, Los Angeles, CA

Grammy-winning singer-songwriter Ben Harper reunites with his longtime backing band The Innocent Criminals for an intimate evening of soulful rock. Their fusion of folk, blues and reggae has captivated audiences around the world, and this special performance at the Orpheum promises a career-spanning set of hits and fan favorites.

The historic Orpheum Theatre provides the perfect backdrop for Harper's heartfelt lyrics and the band's dynamic musicianship. Arrive early to explore downtown Los Angeles' vibrant dining scene before settling into your seat for an unforgettable concert.
