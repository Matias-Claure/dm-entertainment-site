King Gizzard & The Lizard Wizard

Phantom Island Tour - Hollywood Bowl

Event Details

Date: Sunday, August 10, 2025

Time: 7:00 PM (Gate opens at 5:30 PM)

Price: Varies by seating

Location: Hollywood Bowl, Los Angeles, CA

The wildly imaginative and unstoppably prolific Australian rockers return to Los Angeles as part of the KCRW Festival. King Gizzard & The Lizard Wizard join forces with the Hollywood Bowl Orchestra, performing music from their forthcoming album Phantom Island and taking an orchestral stroll through their remarkably diverse discography.

Known for blending psych, prog, thrash, folk and more, this band promises an evening of genre-defying music under the stars. Arrive early to enjoy the pre-concert wine and beer tastings available on site.
